Miami-Dade County
Inspector Feedback

This is a pilot program that allows homeowners and contractors to give constructive feedback to Miami-Dade County inspectors.

Learn more »

We'd like to give you a way for you to give feedback when you go through your upcoming inspections.

Each time you are visited by a Miami-Dade County Inspector, you will be given a link to a feedback survey about his/her performance via e-mail or text message.

This feedback will be aggregated and sent to management about inspector performance.
